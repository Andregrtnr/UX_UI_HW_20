Homi Mobile App

A mobile app connecting LGBT with safe housing options.

RESEARCH

As a team of queer designers, we are all-too aware of the difficulties that we may face because of our identities.In those instances, it is almost imperative to have a network of other LGBTQ+ people to fall back on, and this app aims to provide that network to our users by providing a medium to chat, make friends, and find a dream apartment.

Our team conducted a total of 6 user interviews and sent out a Google survey to determine how LGBTQ+ individuals approach their searches for rooms and roommates. The interviews and the surveying took place over the course of a few days, beginning January 9th. Through our findings we constructed an affinity diagram and an empathy map to reflect the needs of real users.

PROBLEM STATEMENT

As UX researchers, we believe creating an interactive platform directed towards pairing roommates in the LGBTQ+ community with similar personality traits will provide them a safer and more secure way to find affordable housing. .
